Privacy Policy for AdQuadrant Plura App

AdQuadrant (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data collected through the AdQuadrant application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data submitted via TikTok Lead Generation Instant Forms.

1. Information We Collect

When users submit TikTok Lead Generation forms, the App may collect the following types of information on behalf of advertisers:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Other form responses
2. How We Use the Information
  • To transmit lead form data securely to the advertiser’s CRM, marketing, or analytics systems.
  • To enable advertisers to follow up with leads and manage their marketing campaigns.
  • To ensure the App functions as intended and provide technical support.

We do not use the data for our own marketing purposes, nor do we sell or rent this data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing
  • Data is shared only with the advertiser or business account that owns the TikTok ad campaign.
  • We do not disclose lead data to unaffiliated third parties.
  • We may share data if required by law, regulation, or legal request.
4. Data Security
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
5. Data Retention
  • Lead data is retained only as long as necessary to deliver it to the advertiser’s connected systems.
  • Advertisers are responsible for managing and deleting lead data within their CRM or database according to applicable laws.
6. User Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, please contact the advertiser that collected your information or reach out to us directly.
